Following the success of the world renowned restauarant, Best Damn Steakhouse, Nomi's joins the culiary scene to offer the same delightful steaks and potatoes as her sister restaurant but with a twist.



Nomi's Grill proudly serves our potatoes fried.



We know there will be those who feel as if this is a breaking of the trust we have built with our community over the years. That is why we have opened Nomi's Grill - to avoid this sense of betrayl by offering non-mashed potatoes at our original establishment. Nothing has changed at The Best Damn Steakhouse. We are simply offering another option.



We look forward to seeing you soon for more of the best damn steak around and some freshly fried potatoes!
